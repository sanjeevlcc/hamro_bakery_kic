Our Story

Tradition Meets Passion

Started in 2015, The Daily Crumb began as a small family passion project. Today, we remain dedicated to the old-world art of baking. We ferment our sourdough for 24 hours, source organic local flour, and never use artificial preservatives.

Every croissant layer, every pinch of sugar, and every loaf of bread gets our undivided attention so you taste pure happiness in every single bite.
